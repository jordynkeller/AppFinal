Health benefits of water

Water is your body's principal chemical component and makes up about 60% of your body weight. Your body depends on water to survive

Every cell, tissue and organ in your body needs water to work properly. For example, water;

  • Gets ride of wastes through urination, perspiration and bowel movement
  • Keeps your temperature normal
  • Lubricates and cushions joints
  • Protects sensitive tissues

Lack of water can lead to dehydration -- a condition that occurs when you don't have enough water in your body to carry out normal function. Even mild dehydration can drain your energy and make you tired.

(Source: Mayo Clinic Staff)
